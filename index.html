Our Story

Built from
real life.

North Shore Designs didn't start in a boardroom. It started at a hospital bedside. Our founder moved to Boston to be close to his brother — who was fighting a chronic illness — and spent months watching the same thing play out: understaffed nurses, systems that couldn't keep up, care that fell through the cracks. He couldn't sit still. He built a web app to help. Then a mobile app. That's just who he is — in every corporate job he ever had, he was the one who figured it out. Installing the tracking snippet nobody else would touch, learning to code when the situation called for it, wiring things together until they worked. Always finding a way.

When he turned that same energy toward building his own website, something clicked. Every business needs this. Every small business owner is out there doing everything — and most of them have a website that doesn't do them justice. That realization didn't let go.

He lived in Boston. Then Brookline. And then he found the North Shore — and became one with it. The towns, the water, the people who grind every day and actually give a damn about their community. This is home now. And while he builds for businesses everywhere, the North Shore is where his heart is, and where he wants to be most deeply rooted.

He is a small business owner, just like you. He has always found a way — and he will always find a way for you. Pricing will never be a barrier, because this was never about the money.

"In every job I ever had, I was the one who figured it out — the snippet, the integration, the thing nobody else wanted to touch. I watched my brother not get the care he deserved because systems failed him. I'm not going to let a broken website be the reason your business doesn't get the attention it deserves. I have always found a way. I will always find a way for you."

— Founder, North Shore Designs
